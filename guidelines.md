### 🕋 Scanning Rules for Volunteers

#### 1. Before You Scan
* **Use Color:** Set your Adobe Scan filter to **"Original Color"**. *Black & white scans/photocopies are rejected.*
* **Clean Margins:** Place documents on a flat, dark background so the app edges crop cleanly.

#### 2. The Golden Scanning Sequence
Scan the entire cover group into **one single PDF file** in this exact order:

1. **Passports First:** * Cover Head Passport Page 1 → Page 2
   * Co-Pilgrim 1 Passport Page 1 → Page 2
   * Co-Pilgrim 2 Passport Page 1 → Page 2...
2. **Photographs Second:**
   * Cover Head Photo → Co-Pilgrim 1 Photo → Co-Pilgrim 2 Photo...
3. **Bank Details Last:**
   * Scan **only one** Bank Cheque for the entire family group at the very end.

#### 3. Page Count Cheat Sheet
* **1 Pilgrim:** 4 pages total
* **2 Pilgrims:** 7 pages total
* **3 Pilgrims:** 10 pages total
* **4 Pilgrims:** 13 pages total
* **5 Pilgrims:** 16 pages total
